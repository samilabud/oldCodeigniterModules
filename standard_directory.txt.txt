Standard structure for each module
------------------------------------

Module_name (dir)

	->css (dir)
	    ->module_name (dir)

	->js (dir)
	    ->module_name (dir)

	->images (dir)
	    ->module_name (dir)

	->system (dir)
		->application (dir)
			->views (dir)
			->controllers (dir)
			->models (dir)
			->... (dirs)

	->modules_info (dir)
		->module_name (dir)
			->readme.txt (file)
			->script.sql (file)
			->... (files)2
			->... (files)
